MY FAMILY

I come from a close-knit family with my father, Ahmad Azhari, who worked as a dedicated farmer, and my mother, Rozilah, a nurturing housewife. Our household was always lively and filled with love, especially with my grandma's wisdom and warmth adding to the mix. I have an older brother who often took on the role of protector and guide, and two younger brothers who kept the home bustling with their energy and mischief. Growing up in this vibrant and supportive family environment shaped me into who I am today, instilling in me values of hard work, care, and togetherness.
